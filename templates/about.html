About

This web app is designed to translate communication between two different languages, enabling someone to provide directions to another individual to a building on the campus of the University of Florida.

It is built using Flask, HTML, JavaScript, and CSS. It relies on Google Cloud's Translation API and LeafletJS for the map. The map tiles implemented are OpenStreetMap, and UF's official overlay.
